Controls

  • Left / Right moves the falling item between bucket lanes
  • 1, 2, 3, 4 snaps straight to Incident, Alert, Service Request, Problem
  • Up or Space flips the card around for a clue view
  • Down quick drops the card
  • P pauses or resumes

Bucket Logic

  • Incident means something is broken or degraded right now
  • Alert means monitoring triggered, but not always user impact yet
  • Service Request means a standard ask, access, change, or provisioning task
  • Problem means underlying cause, trend, repeat issue, or root cause investigation

Tip

  • Correct placements reduce pressure by slightly slowing the fall rate
  • Wrong placements speed things up and cost a life
  • Longer streaks earn more points
  • Using clues reduces points
